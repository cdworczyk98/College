Choosing a Testing Approach

After we have decided on a test strategy, we need to decide what kind of tests would be used within the strategy, to best increase confidence in the artefact (whether software or a network installation). That is, we must now decide on a category of tests.

We will look at two of the most common categories of testing:

Black box testing (sometimes known as functional testing).

Glass box, or white box testing (sometimes known as structural testing).
